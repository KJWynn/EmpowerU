Welcome to an introductory course to Artificial Intelligence!

The objective of this unit is to provide a foundational understanding of Artificial Intelligence and its various applications. 
Throughout the course, you will gain knowledge of AI principles, including machine learning, neural networks, and ethical 
concerns. Each lesson will outline learning goals and objectives for the week, helping you track your progress. No prior 
programming or AI experience is required, and we encourage students with diverse backgrounds to engage with the material and 
collaborate with their peers.

On completion of the unit you will be able to:
- Describe what AI is and explain the core concepts related to AI
- Demonstrate how AI applications and use cases can transform our lives and our work
- Recognize the potential and impact of AI to transform businesses and careers
- Describe the issues, limitations, and ethical concerns surrounding AI
